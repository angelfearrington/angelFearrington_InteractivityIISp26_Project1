Why This Is Important

I know that a lot of people struggle to keep up with the news for various reasons.



A List of Commonly Stated Reasons:
  1. Being too busy in your own daily life

  2. The news is too depressing and heavy to pay attention to

  3. There is always things constantly happening all over the world making it too difficult to keep up

  4. Believing that these things do not effect you

Overall, all of these statements can be very true in their own rights. However, even though the news might seem like its too much to handle, it is still very important to at least try to be aware of what is happening that may be affecting your friends, family, neighbors, and community members, as well as in other cultures and societies.
