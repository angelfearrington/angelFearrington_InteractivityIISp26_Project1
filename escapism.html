ESCAPISM

On this final page, I want to take a step back from all of these heavy topics in our daily news cycle and focus on some more lighthearted stuff!

Here I want to share the unserious types of content I view! Even though I enjoy the news based content and it is the content I have the most time to view, sometimes I need a break from it for my mental health. And when I need this break I usually turn to: Gaming, Minecraft, K-pop, Music, Drama/Tea Channels, Commentary, and Comedy Videos.

Click around on some videos and give them a watch! I hope you enjoy these and they take a load off of your shoulders even for a little while!!
